Jonesboro Parks & Recreation

Southside Softball Complex

5003 Stadium BLVD, Jonesboro AR 72404

In 1985, a group known as the Jonesboro Softball Association was trying to develop the Southside
Softball Complex into one of the finest recreational facilities in the south and the program has grown
ever since. In 1987, Jonesboro Parks and Recreation was given oversight authority for any organizations
utilizing or managing the city park facilities. The city has completed many upgrades to the complex since
its inception and long range upgrades continue to be a priority.

• Lighted complex
• Paved parking
• 4 adult softball fields
• 5 youth fields
• Restroom facilities
• Concessions
