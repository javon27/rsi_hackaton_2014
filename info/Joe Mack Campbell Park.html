Jonesboro Parks & Recreation

Joe Mack Campbell Park

3021 Dan Ave, Jonesboro AR 72401

Joe Mack Campbell, for whom the park is named, formally opened in the spring of 2002. Jo Mack Campbell
and his wife had always been interested in helping youth in the community. When another park was needed,
Mr. Campbell gladly offered his land in hopes of providing an establishment for sporting events for
children. This 110 acre Sporting Complex offers sporting recreational activities for the community to
enjoy.

• 14 lighted youth baseball fields
• 21 soccer fields
• Tennis courts
• Playground
• Pavilions
• Restroom facilities
• Walking track
• Lighted complex, paved parking
• Concessions
