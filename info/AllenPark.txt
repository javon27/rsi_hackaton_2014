Allen Park and Community Center
3609 Race Street, Jonesboro AR 72401

The Allen family moved to Nettleton from Memphis, Tennessee in 1912. At that time, they purchased a portion of the Allen land. Over the years, the family acquired more land surrounding the area. In 2002, George Allen donated the twenty-three acres of land where Allen Park now sits. The official park dedication and opening was on June 28, 2002. Allen Park offers the following amenities:
•	Pavilion
•	Picnic area
•	Restrooms
•	Play-grounds
•	Skate park
•	Soccer/football fields
•	Tennis courts
•	Walking track
•	Splash pad
•	Community center with a catering kitchen, a gymnasium, and two additional rooms available for renting.
•	Paved parking, lighted complex
