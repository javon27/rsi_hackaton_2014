Earl Bell Community Center
1212 South Church, Jonesboro AR 72401

What used to be known as the Church Street Community Center was formally re-named the Earl Bell Community Center in 1984. The name change is in honor of Earl Bell, who was the first American to clear 19-feet in the pole vault outdoors and was the first 19-year old ever to pole vault 18-feet. Earl Bell also won a gold medal in pole vaulting at the 1975 Pan American games and set the world record, which he held for 24-days3 He was ranked number 1 in the world in 1977. The Earl Bell Community Center offers the following amenities:
•	3 meeting rooms are available for reservation
•	Rock climbing wall
•	Tennis courts
•	Gymnasium 
•	Outdoor Julian James Park
